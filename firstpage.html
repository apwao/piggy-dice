Rules of the game

  1. Choose a player to go first. Player throws a die and scores as many points as the total shown on the die providing the die doesnâ€™t roll a 1.
  2. Player continues rolling and accumulating points at risk of rolling a 1 or holds.
  3. If player rolls a 1 his turn is over, he loses all points accumulated during that turn, he passes the die.
  4. Play passes from player to player until a winner is determined.
  5. The first player to accumulate 100 or more points wins the game.
